Design Brief Questions:

  • Who are you? - I am a Multidisciplinary Designer/Creative
  • Your Objective - To present myself professionally without losing out on my character/personality
  • How would you like to be perceived - I’d like to be seen as someone who can get things done but is flat or boring
  • Who is your audience - Primarily potential employers, but also potential one-off customers for commision work.
  • Who is your competitor - Every other young creative is constantly trying to get work, I need to stand out
  • What defines a successful project? - I want to look at a finished product and know it's mine. Not quite a signature, but an identifier.
  • What do you want this to say about you? - Most importantly, I want this to be to the point I don’t want to appear overcomplicated.
  • What is your favourite color - Orange/Red
  • Keywords - Fun, Different, Clever
